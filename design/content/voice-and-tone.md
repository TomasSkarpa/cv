## Voice and tone

Copy comes from `content/textations/`. UI chrome (labels, errors, aria) stays plain and direct.

### Voice principles

- **First person, short sentences**: Say what you do, where, and with what. No adjectives unless they carry information.
- **Specific over impressive**: "SFCC storefronts at Bata Group" beats "scalable online retail systems."
- **Dry wit is fine**: One understated line beats three paragraphs of enthusiasm.
- **Human, not LinkedIn**: Write like you'd explain your job to someone at a pub in Zlín.

### UI chrome

- Navigation and buttons: short noun labels (`CV`, `Projects`, `Email me`).
- Form errors: verbatim from `contact.md` (`Please enter your name.`, `Use a valid email address.`, `Please write a message.`).
- Status: `Opening your email client…` on contact submit.

### Tone by context

| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Home hero | Name, role, place. One fact, one aside. |
| How I work / CV | Outcome-focused, no filler |
| Outside work | Specific interests, no "passion" |
| Projects | Role, context, contribution, outcome |
| Errors | Calm, specific, no blame |

### Avoid

- "Passionate about", "innovative", "scalable", "enhancing user experience"
- Generic portfolio phrasing not in textations
- Jargon without context
- Em dashes in new UI copy
